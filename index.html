What is Gender Equality?

Gender equality is the equal rights, responsibilities, and opportunities of women, men, girls, and boys. It implies that the interests, needs, and priorities of each gender are respected equally and do not determine their roles and responsibilities in society and private life.

Why Gender Equality Matters

Gender equality is not only a fundamental human right, but a necessary foundation for a peaceful, prosperous, and sustainable world. Gender equality is essential for economies and communities to thrive. When women are empowered, whole societies benefit.
